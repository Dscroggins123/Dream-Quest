Rules

  • Select your custom Hero from the "Choose Character" button in the Nav Bar at the top of the page
  • Click the "Begin" button to load your custom hero to dreamquestlogo board
  • Roll the dice to move your Hero along the board
  • After you roll you will be redirected to a game page where you will Answer a Riddle, Play a mini game , or open a gift.
  • If you answer the riddle correctly or complete the mini game your hero will be leveled up by 25 Hitpoints(HP) and 20 Attack Points. Answer incorrectly and receive no boost.
  • Advance through your quest and acquire as many Hitpoints(HP) and Attack Points as possible.
  • When you get to the end of the Quest you will fight the final boss Nogara to restore peace to the land.
  • If you haven't acquired enought HP and Attack Points you will be defeated and will be prompted to take the Quest again

Good luck Hero the future depends on you !!
